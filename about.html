About Felix Tree Service.

Founded in 1981 and proudly owned by Felix Salazar, we are a specialized division of White Plains Landscaping, Inc. dedicated to the highest standards of arboriculture and property stewardship.

Rooted in the Community

For over four decades, we have been the trusted caretakers of the suburban canopy across Westchester County and beyond. Our philosophy marries the rugged demands of technical tree work with the refined expectations of high-end property maintenance.

Whether serving a private homeowner looking to protect a generational oak, or a commercial client managing an expansive corporate campus, our approach remains the same: meticulous planning, uncompromising safety, and a deep respect for the natural environment. We don't just remove trees; we manage living landscapes.
